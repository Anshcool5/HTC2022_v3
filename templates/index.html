Some info about our project

About

We're scraping the daily menu options for the variuos meals available at our dinning hall. A student will then be prompted to enter some quantitative variables such as their height, weight, age, etc. This will help our program to determine the perfect meal for them and ensure no wastage and under-eating users.

Aim

Our program aims to promote sutainable use of food prepared at buffet systems along with promoting healthy eating habits among users.

Future Plans

In the future, we hope to -
1). Add functionality for more buffet systems throughout the country.
2). Accomodate users with dietary restrictions in the future.
3). Reduce food wastage at buffet restaurants by providing meal plans of potential customers before food preparation begins.
